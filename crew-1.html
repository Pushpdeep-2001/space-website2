Wing Commander

Rakesh Sharma

Rakesh Sharma, AC (born 13 January 1949) is a former Indian Air Force pilot who flew aboard Soyuz T-11 on 3 April 1984. India also conferred its highest peacetime gallantry award, the Ashoka Chakra, to him.
